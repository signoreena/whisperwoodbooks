*Trim size: 5.06" × 7.81" (I Can Read / Frog and Toad format). Portrait orientation. No bleed. Vignette edges: central action in middle third, edges dissolve softly to white. Sharp editorial linework with warmth — Quentin Blake energy meets Edward Ardizzone warmth. 7 chapters — 9 illustration spreads plus cover. This is the series finale — every lesson, every character, every resource returns.*
