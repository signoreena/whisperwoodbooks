## Spread 9: The Whole Long Path Lit Warm — Final Closing Spread

Children's picture book interior illustration, 5.06 by 7.81 inches portrait, no bleed. Sharp editorial linework with warmth — Quentin Blake meets Edward Ardizzone. This is the final image of the Whisperwood series. A long forest path, lined on both sides with lantern mushrooms glowing warm amber-gold, stretching from the foreground into the distance — the path disappearing into old-growth forest in a warm vanishing point. Walking along it: Vera, Pip, and Sage. Vera walks with her bag on her shoulder and her list folded small and tucked away. Pip walks between them — for once not ahead, not mid-leap, just walking with them, one hand in his pocket and something settled in his expression. Sage walks on the far side, one-step-at-a-time, the steadiest she has ever looked. The path is long and the mushroom light is everywhere. The canopy above is dappled with the last of the afternoon. There is no crisis. There is no problem to solve. There is only the path home, lit warm, and the three of them on it together. The entire weight of eight books lives in this image. Edges dissolve softly to white — softer here than in any previous spread.
