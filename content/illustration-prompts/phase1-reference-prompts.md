# Whisperwood — Phase 1 Reference Generation Prompts

Generate these in order. Each one becomes a reference image you attach to subsequent prompts.
Style to maintain throughout: warm watercolor and ink children's book illustration, soft vignette edges dissolving to white, earthy forest palette, golden dappled light.

---

## WORLD & LOCATIONS

---

### W1: Forest World — Palette Reference

No characters. This establishes the visual world for the entire series.

Warm watercolor and ink children's book illustration. Old-growth deciduous forest in full leaf, late morning. Wide canopy of maple and oak, golden light filtering through in soft columns. Mossy ground with exposed roots, small wildflowers at the base of tree trunks. A gentle forest path winds into the middle distance. Deep forest-green foliage, warm gold light, rich earth tones. Lantern mushrooms — small golden-glowing fungi — grow in clusters near the roots. No characters. The scene should feel settled and whole, as if it has been this way for a very long time. Soft vignette: edges dissolve gently to white, no hard border. Portrait orientation.

---

### W2: The River & Talking Rock

The series' most important location. Generate after W1 — attach W1 as style reference.

Warm watercolor and ink children's book illustration, style consistent with attached forest reference. A river bend in old-growth forest, water calm and slightly high. Left bank has a large ancient mossy boulder — the Talking Rock — half-buried in the bank, rounded and settled, weight and age in every line. Thick soft moss covers its surface. Exposed oak roots frame the bank. Bellflowers hang from a low vine nearby. The water reflects gold and green. No characters. Late afternoon light, amber quality. Soft vignette edges dissolving to white. Portrait orientation.

---

### W3: The Forest Path at Night

The closing image of every book. Generate after W1 — attach W1 as style reference.

Warm watercolor and ink children's book illustration, style consistent with attached forest reference. A forest path at night, the canopy arching overhead like a tunnel of old branches. Lantern mushrooms line both sides of the path in soft golden clusters, casting warm pools of light on the ground. Stars barely visible through the canopy. The path curves gently into the middle distance, warm and inviting, not dark or scary. The overall feeling: a long night that held, and now everyone is walking home. No characters. Soft vignette edges dissolving to white. Portrait orientation.

---

### W4: The Village & Big Oak

The community gathering place. Generate after W1 — attach W1 as style reference.

Warm watercolor and ink children's book illustration, style consistent with attached forest reference. A forest village clearing: a great old oak at the far end, its roots spreading wide across the ground. Small tunnel entrances visible at the base of the oak and along the clearing's edge. A bellflower vine hangs from a low branch. The clearing is open enough to gather a community but ringed with old trees. Daytime, dappled light. Evidence of small animal life: worn paths between the roots, a small stack of gathered materials near a burrow entrance. No characters. Soft vignette edges dissolving to white. Portrait orientation.
