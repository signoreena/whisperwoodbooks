Illustration 9 — COMMUNITY IMAGE
The whole community gathered at the river’s edge at night, singing. LEFT HALF — foreground: Pip with everything he has, slightly off-note but giving his whole body to it. Vera at the front, conducting, humming the first note. The mud at the bank beginning to shift left, a new channel forming. RIGHT HALF — the crowd: Bram and two beavers; a family of deer with heads lowered, singing low. Three raccoons. The otter from the slide — arms still half-crossed but mouth open and singing. Sage at the very back of the crowd, one quiet step forward, singing exactly the right note, barely visible, knowing what her part is. Glowmoss lighting the scene from below.
