Illustration 12 — FINAL SPREAD
The trio walking home on a dappled forest path in morning light. Pip leads, holding both lantern mushrooms up like torches — completely unnecessarily, it is full day. Vera and Sage walk together behind him. Forest warm, exhaled. In the far background between the trees: the mouse family’s temporary shelter. Ma Mouse hanging their small framed painting on the exterior wall. Pa on a stump, baby asleep in his arms. Big Daughter writing on the step. Middle Son watching the trio walk away, still hoping something interesting will happen. Nothing will. Not today.
Replace with final artwork — this image echoes all eight books. The mouse family’s window will be lit in Book 8.

He held up both lantern mushrooms and marched off down the path toward home.

Vera watched him go. It had been Pip who ran when she needed him to. Who had gathered every creature who could sing, just because she’d asked. His legs had carried the whole plan to everyone who needed to be part of it. That was the part no plan could plan for.

Vera looked at Sage. Sage looked at Vera. They followed him.
