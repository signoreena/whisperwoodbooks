Illustration 9 — COMMUNITY IMAGE
The whole community gathered at the river’s edge at night, singing. Pip foreground, everything he has, slightly off-note but whole-body committed. Vera at the front conducting, humming the first note. The mud at the bank beginning to shift left, a new channel forming, lantern mushrooms lighting it from below. Bram and two beavers; a family of deer with heads lowered, singing low. Three raccoons. The otter from the slide — arms still half-crossed but mouth open and singing. Sage at the very back of the crowd, one quiet step forward, singing exactly the right note.
