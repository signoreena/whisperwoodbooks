Illustration 9 — FINAL SPREAD: Walking Home
The closing image of Book 2, echoing the series’ first. A forest path at night in Whisperwood — deep blue-green canopy above, two rows of amber lantern mushrooms growing along both path edges, lighting the way home. Pip walks half a step ahead at center — tail up, expression warm and present, listening to the quiet that surrounds him, not leaping, not carrying anything. Beside him on the right, Vera walks with her satchel on her shoulder and a folded list in her paw she is not looking at — ears forward, expression of someone who has learned something that will change how she asks questions next time. On the left, Sage walks one deliberate step at a time, looking ahead down the path — her expression the quietest it has been in the entire book, genuinely at rest. In the upper-left background, the forked elm is visible — a single small songbird folds its wings in the fork, asleep. In the far right background, a warm amber light glows in the bakery window — Hazel starting her dough.
