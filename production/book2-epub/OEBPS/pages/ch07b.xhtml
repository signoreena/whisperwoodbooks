Chapter 7
The Night Grows Quiet

Oswin knew where the quietcap mushrooms grew.

Of course he did.

He led them to the quietcap grove — Vera, Pip, Sage, two apprentice owls, and the same three raccoons who had tried the eastern fork. The mushrooms were pale and round. Each one absorbed sound the way wool absorbs water: quietly, completely.

“Three locations,” said Vera. She looked at Sage. “You’re certain?”

“Yes,” said Sage.

“You stayed in the noise to find where it crossed,” said Vera. “I should have asked you first. Before the schedule plan.”

“You didn’t know what I could hear,” said Sage.

“I didn’t ask,” said Vera.

“Let’s place them,” said Sage.
