WHISPERWOOD BOOK 2 — ILLUSTRATION DROP FOLDER
==============================================

Drop your finished illustration files here.
Once all (or some) images are in place, tell Claude and the placement
script will copy them into the ePub and print files automatically.


NAMING CONVENTION
-----------------
Name files exactly as shown. The script matches by filename.

  cover.jpg       Front cover art — Sage alone at forked elm, canopy erupting in visual noise above her
  illo-01.jpg     Ch 1 — OPENING SPREAD: Whisperwood at rest; songbird relay running; Sage's lit window
  illo-02.jpg     Ch 2 — Pip crouches at Hazel's closed bakery; Hazel on step, exhausted, apron untied
  illo-03.jpg     Ch 3 — Vera mid-stride toward canopy, Pip running to keep up, list in paw
  illo-04.jpg     Ch 4 — Library tree interior; Vera before Oswin, ears back, list folded in half
  illo-05.jpg     Ch 5 — Pip on Sage's step, stick beside him; Sage hands flat on boards, almost-smiling
  illo-06.jpg     Ch 6 — KEY SPREAD: Sage at forked elm, hand on bark, jaw set; three relay chains crossing
  illo-07.jpg     Ch 7 — Oswin leads group through quietcap mushroom grove; Sage breathing at grove edge
  illo-08.jpg     Ch 7 — Final cluster placed at path bend; canopy thinning; Sage leads, Vera follows
  illo-09.jpg     Ch 7 — FINAL SPREAD: trio walks home at night, lantern mushrooms, forked elm sleeping


FILE FORMAT
-----------
  Format:   JPG or PNG (JPG preferred for final art — smaller file size)
  Color:    RGB (not CMYK — KDP handles conversion)
  Bit depth: 8-bit


SIZE REQUIREMENTS
-----------------
Single-page illustrations (illos 02, 03, 05, 07):
  Minimum for ebook:  1700 × 1700 px  (200 ppi at 8.5")
  Minimum for print:  2625 × 2625 px  (300 ppi at 8.75" with bleed)
  → If generating at print size (2625×2625), the ebook copy will scale down fine.

Full-spread illustrations (illos 01, 04, 06, 08, 09) — TWO PAGES WIDE:
  Minimum for ebook:  3400 × 1700 px  (200 ppi across two 8.5" pages)
  Minimum for print:  5250 × 2625 px  (300 ppi across two 8.75" pages)
  → Same file used for both formats. The print layout crops each half
    onto its own page automatically.

Cover (cover.jpg) — single square page:
  Minimum:  2625 × 2625 px  (300 ppi at 8.75" with bleed)
  Note: KDP also requires a separate cover file for the back cover +
  spine. That is handled via KDP's Cover Creator tool at upload time.
  This file is the FRONT COVER only, used inside the ebook as well.


SPREAD NOTES
------------
COVER: The visual argument of the whole book in one image — chaos
above the elm fork (overlapping songbird relay arcs in amber and
orange), absolute stillness below (Sage, hand flat on bark, jaw set,
eyes closed). The break between the two zones happens exactly at the
elm fork where Sage's hand is placed. At thumbnail size this must read
immediately: loud above, still below.

illo-06 (KEY SPREAD) is the book's emotionally critical full-spread.
Three relay chains — arriving from upper-left, upper-right, and far
center — must visibly converge at the elm fork. The visual argument is
the mechanism: the reader should understand from the image alone why
this is the crossing point and why Sage's hand is here. The mouse
family is unusually foregrounded in this spread: Pa's stillness echoes
Sage's, Middle Son looks directly up at her, Ma holds the framed
painting to her chest.

illo-04 is a full-bleed double-page spread: the interior of the library
tree dwarfs both Vera and Oswin — the scale is the point. Vera's ears
back (the involuntary tell) + the folded list are her two key physical
tells in Book 2. The folded list is important: she has not put it away,
she has folded it in half. She is not ready to abandon it; she just
cannot look at it right now.

illo-08 is a full-bleed double-page spread: the final quietcap cluster
placed at the path bend; canopy thinning above; Sage leads with
certainty while Vera follows.

illo-09 (FINAL SPREAD) is the Book 2 instance of the series' closing
image. It must be recognizably the same composition as Book 1's final
spread (path, trio, lantern mushrooms, home ahead) with Book 2's
specific notes: it is night, not morning; Pip is not carrying anything
(no mushrooms held aloft); the forked elm is visible upper-left with a
single bird folded in sleep; the bakery window glows in the far right.
Trio expressions are one degree forward from Book 1: Vera changed
(not catching herself), Sage at rest (not quietly vindicated), Pip
present (not exuberant).


MOUSE FAMILY ARC IN BOOK 2
---------------------------
Temporary shelter near the forked elm, above the owl's library tree.
Appears in: cover (background), illo-01, illo-06, illo-08, illo-09.

  cover      Background note only — Pa with Baby asleep, framed painting propped
  illo-01    Settling into shelter for what will be a loud night; Middle Son's head tilted toward canopy
  illo-06    Most foregrounded appearance — Pa calm echoing Sage; Middle Son looking directly up at her
  illo-08    Middle Son and Big Daughter both looking up at thinning relay; Pa calm, Baby asleep
  illo-09    Family settled inside for the night; painting on the bark wall; Middle Son not at entrance

Baby is asleep in all spreads.


PARTIAL DROPS ARE FINE
-----------------------
You don't need all 10 files at once. Drop what you have, tell Claude,
and the script will place whatever is available. Missing images stay
as placeholders. Run placement again when more images are ready.


HOW TO TRIGGER PLACEMENT
-------------------------
1. Drop image files here (named exactly as above)
2. Commit and push to git
3. Tell Claude: "images are ready, please place them"
4. Claude runs: production/place-illustrations.sh
5. Both formats update automatically. ePub is rebuilt and ready to upload.
