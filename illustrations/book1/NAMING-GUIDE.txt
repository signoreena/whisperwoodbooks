WHISPERWOOD BOOK 1 — ILLUSTRATION DROP FOLDER
==============================================

Drop your finished illustration files here.
Once all (or some) images are in place, tell Claude and the placement
script will copy them into the ePub and print files automatically.


NAMING CONVENTION
-----------------
Name files exactly as shown. The script matches by filename.

  cover.jpg       Front cover art
  illo-01.jpg     Ch 1 — Ordinary Tuesday establishing shot (Vera writes, Pip leaps, Sage watches tadpoles)
  illo-02.jpg     Ch 1 — Mapleaf veins go dark; Pip falls toward Vera
  illo-03.jpg     Ch 2 — Sage alone at rising river, bellflower mid-ring
  illo-04.jpg     Ch 3/4 — Pip leans into useless stick; Sage perfectly still beside him
  illo-05.jpg     Ch 4 — KEY SPREAD: Sage hands on ancient boulder, Pip nose-to-rock
  illo-06.jpg     Ch 4 — Vera's ears go back before Bram at the beaver dam
  illo-07.jpg     Ch 7 — COMMUNITY SPREAD: whole forest singing to the mud, channel forming
  illo-08.jpg     Ch 8 — Beavers working the fallen oak at dawn; river beginning to run free
  illo-09.jpg     Ch 8 — Mouse family in above-ground shelter; Ma hanging framed painting
  illo-10.jpg     Ch 9 — FINAL SPREAD: trio walks home, lantern mushrooms, mouse family shelter in background


FILE FORMAT
-----------
  Format:   JPG or PNG (JPG preferred for final art — smaller file size)
  Color:    RGB (not CMYK — KDP handles conversion)
  Bit depth: 8-bit


SIZE REQUIREMENTS
-----------------
Single-page illustrations (illos 02, 04, 06, 08):
  Minimum for ebook:  1700 × 1700 px  (200 ppi at 8.5")
  Minimum for print:  2625 × 2625 px  (300 ppi at 8.75" with bleed)
  → If generating at print size (2625×2625), the ebook copy will scale down fine.

Full-spread illustrations (illos 01, 03, 05, 07, 09, 10) — TWO PAGES WIDE:
  Minimum for ebook:  3400 × 1700 px  (200 ppi across two 8.5" pages)
  Minimum for print:  5250 × 2625 px  (300 ppi across two 8.75" pages)
  → Same file used for both formats. The print layout crops each half
    onto its own page automatically.

Cover (cover.jpg) — single square page:
  Minimum:  2625 × 2625 px  (300 ppi at 8.75" with bleed)
  Note: KDP also requires a separate cover file for the back cover +
  spine. That is handled via KDP's Cover Creator tool at upload time.
  This file is the FRONT COVER only, used inside the ebook as well.


SPREAD NOTES
------------
illo-05 (KEY SPREAD) and illo-07 (COMMUNITY SPREAD) are the two
emotionally critical full-spreads. If generating in batches, prioritize
these. The key visual in illo-05: Sage's hands flat on the boulder,
eyes closed; Pip's nose millimeters from the rock, holding his breath.

illo-03 is also a full-bleed double-page spread: Sage alone at the
rising river, holding the bellflower mid-ring. The river's wrong-ness
is visible but not yet dramatic — still water, just too high.

illo-09 is a full-bleed double-page spread: the mouse family in their
above-ground shelter, Ma hanging the framed painting on the bark wall.

illo-06 contains Vera's most important physical character moment in
Book 1: her ears going back (not flat, just moved from their usual
forward-alert position) as she stands before Bram. Must read clearly
but subtly — she is caught, not defeated.

illo-08 (beavers at the fallen oak): the root ball of the fallen oak
must show old, weathered damage — this detail pays off in Book 8.
Show it clearly enough that a reader returning to Book 1 after Book 8
would notice it.

illo-10 (FINAL SPREAD) establishes the series' closing visual: trio
on the path, lantern mushrooms lining both edges, home ahead. This
composition recurs in every subsequent book. The Mapleaf tree in the
far background should show veins returned to gold-green — bookend to
the dark veins of illo-02.


PARTIAL DROPS ARE FINE
-----------------------
You don't need all 11 files at once. Drop what you have, tell Claude,
and the script will place whatever is available. Missing images stay
as placeholders. Run placement again when more images are ready.


HOW TO TRIGGER PLACEMENT
-------------------------
1. Drop image files here (named exactly as above)
2. Commit and push to git
3. Tell Claude: "images are ready, please place them"
4. Claude runs: production/place-illustrations.sh
5. Both formats update automatically. ePub is rebuilt and ready to upload.
